# New Features & Enhancements - ArtisAuc

The following features and improvements have been applied to the ArtisAuc platform to enhance the user experience and provide more value during the auction process.

## 2. Sticky Bid Panel 📌
- **Description**: The bidding panel on the Lot Detail page now stays visible as you scroll through vehicle specs and images.
- **How to Use**: Simply scroll down on any Lot Detail page; the bid form will remain pinned to the right side of the screen.
- **Benefits**: Easier access to place bids without scrolling back to the top.

## 3. Landed Cost Estimator 💰
- **Description**: Real-time estimation of the total cost (Bid + Fees + Shipping) integrated into the Bid Panel.
- **How to Use**: As you enter a Proxy Bid Amount, the estimated total in JPY and USD is automatically calculated and displayed.
- **Benefits**: Provides immediate clarity on the true cost of importing a vehicle.

## 4. Watchlist (Favorites) ❤️
- **Description**: Save vehicles you're interested in for quick access later.
- **How to Use**: Hover over any vehicle card in the results and click the heart icon. 
- **Persistence**: Saved vehicles are stored in your local session and persist across browser refreshes.

## 5. Vehicle Comparison Tool ⚖️
- **Description**: Compare up to 4 vehicles side-by-side.
- **How to Use**: Click the scale icon on vehicle cards to add them to your comparison list. A drawer will appear at the bottom showing your selected items.
- **Benefits**: Evaluate different lots quickly to find the best value.

## 6. Quick Filtering ⚡
- **Description**: Pre-defined filter buttons on the results page for the most common search criteria.
- **Filters**: 
  - **High Grade**: Vehicles with an auction grade of 4.0 or higher.
  - **Low Mileage**: Vehicles with less than 50,000 km.
  - **Closing Soon**: Vehicles prioritized by auction time.
- **How to Use**: Click the filter chips above the search results grid.

## 7. Intelligent Form Validation ✅
- **Description**: Real-time validation for bid submissions.
- **Features**: 
  - Ensures bid amounts meet minimum requirements.
  - Validates that the Maximum Ceiling is greater than or equal to the Proxy Bid.
  - Prevents invalid group selections for late-day auctions.
- **Benefits**: Reduces submission errors and ensures bid integrity.
